Pong Ascension

Controls

Move your mouse up/down to control your paddle (left side).
Score by sending balls past the right paddle.

Boards & Progression

After every 10 rebirths, you unlock a new Pong board, up to 100!
All boards are controlled in sync by your mouse.
Boards arrange in a grid (max 10x10).
